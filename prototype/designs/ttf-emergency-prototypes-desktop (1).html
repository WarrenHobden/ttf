What to do in specific situations

  • Move them to a quiet, calm space
  • Keep them away from bright lights and loud noise
  • Speak calmly and reassuringly
  • Encourage slow, deep breaths
  • Stay with them until they feel better
  • Move them somewhere cool
  • Remove excess clothing
  • Sip water slowly (not too fast)
  • Fan them or use a cool cloth
  • Call 999 if temperature doesn't drop
  • Keep them sitting up if possible
  • Talk to them to keep them awake
  • Don't give them coffee
  • Don't put them in a cold bath
  • If you can't wake them: call 999 immediately

Recovery position

Use this if someone is unconscious but breathing.

Tilt their head back and lift their chin to open the airway

Lie them on their side with their legs straight

Place their nearest arm at right angles to their body

Pull the far leg up and roll them towards you

Tilt head back to keep airway open
